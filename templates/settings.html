How to get your li_at cookie
  1. Open linkedin.com in your browser and log in normally
  2. Press F12 to open Developer Tools
  3. Go to the Application tab (Chrome) or Storage tab (Firefox)
  4. In the left sidebar, expand Cookies → click https://www.linkedin.com
  5. Find the cookie named li_at and copy its Value
  6. Paste it above with a label and click Add Account
